CaffeiMate

Get your drink delivered to you anywhere on U of T St. George Campus

Step 1:

Look at the clock and realize you are only 45 minutes through a 3 hour lecture. Become sad as hell and start yawning profusely.

Step 2:

Hop on to CaffeiMate and order your choice of beverage from Starbucks or Tim Hortons. Note: You must order at least 20 minutes before the hour for your delivery to arrive on the hour.

Step 3:

CaffeiMate will go to Starbucks or Tim Hortons and order your drink, then deliver it to you on the hour. Pay on delivery in cash or credit.
